Returns & Refunds

This policy outlines the guidelines for returns and refunds on QuickSell:

Dispute Resolution

  • Buyers and sellers are encouraged to communicate directly to resolve any issues.
  • If a resolution cannot be reached, contact QuickSell support for assistance.

Conditions for Refunds

  • Item is significantly different from the description.
  • Item is damaged or defective.

Return Process

  • Contact the seller to request a return.
  • If the seller agrees, arrange for the item to be returned.
  • Once the item is received, the refund will be processed.
