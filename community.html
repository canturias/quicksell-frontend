Community Guidelines

To ensure a positive experience for everyone on QuickSell, please adhere to the following guidelines:

Respectful Communication

  • Treat all users with respect and courtesy.
  • Avoid offensive language, harassment, or discrimination.

Accurate Listings

  • Provide accurate descriptions and photos of items for sale.
  • Disclose any defects or issues with the item.

Fair Pricing

  • Set reasonable prices for your items.
  • Be open to negotiation, but avoid price gouging.

Dispute Resolution

  • Attempt to resolve disputes amicably with the other party.
  • If a resolution cannot be reached, contact QuickSell support for assistance.
