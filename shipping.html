Shipping & Delivery

While QuickSell primarily focuses on local transactions, some users may choose to offer or request shipping. Here are some guidelines:

Local Delivery Options

  • Arrange a meeting point for exchange.
  • Consider using local courier services.

Recommended Services

  • Research reputable shipping companies in your area.
  • Compare prices and services before choosing a provider.

Safe Packaging

  • Use appropriate packaging materials to protect the item during transit.
  • Include tracking and insurance for valuable items.
