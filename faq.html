Frequently Asked Questions

What is QuickSell?

QuickSell is an online marketplace designed to make buying and selling items locally quick, easy, and secure.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and following the instructions.

How do I list an item for sale?

Once you have an account, you can list an item by clicking the "Create Listing" button and providing the necessary information about your item.

Is it safe to buy and sell on QuickSell?

We take safety seriously and have implemented measures to protect our users. However, it's important to exercise caution and follow our safety guidelines when buying and selling.
